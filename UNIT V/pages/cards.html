Charmander

Charmander is a Fire-type Pokémon known for its lizard-like appearance. It has a small, orange body with blue eyes and a long, slender tail that flames at the tip, which burns brightly as a sign of its life force. Charmander evolves into Charmeleon and then into Charizard. It's known for its playful and friendly nature but can be fierce in battle. Charmander is a popular choice among trainers.

Squirtle

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokémon known for its small, turtle-like appearance. It has a blue body, large round eyes, and a shell that is typically brown and tan. Squirtle evolves into Wartortle and then into Blastoise. In battles, it uses water-based moves. Squirtle is also recognized for its role as one of the three starter Pokémon from the Kanto region in the Pokémon games.

Stats:
HP:10 Attack: 10

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon characterized by its green, dinosaur-like appearance and the large plant bulb on its back. It evolves into Ivysaur and then into Venusaur. Bulbasaur is known for its friendly disposition and ability to use moves like Vine Whip and Solar Beam. The bulb on its back absorbs sunlight, which helps it grow and powers its abilities.

HP:10 Attack: 10
